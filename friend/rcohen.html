Robin M. Cohen

Former dancer Robin Cohen is an accomplished sculptor in stone, having studied with master sculptor Michele Chapin of Ventura. She also does semi-abstract paintings, custom portraits of favorite sports figures and face painting.

You can see examples of Robin's work on her Robin M. Cohen Website. She's also to be found at Blogspot.com
