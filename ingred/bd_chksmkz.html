Smoked Chicken


Smoked Chicken can serve as a snack or appetizer, but also be used as an ingredient in recipes. The photo specimen was 8-3/4 inches long by 5 inches high and weighed 1.5 pounds. It was produced by Gurman Quality Meats in Mondelein IL. Ing: Chicken, water, salt, cane and maple sugar, sodium phosphate, sodium erythorbate, sodium nitrite, spice extracts.

More on Chickens.

Buying:

  The photo specimen was purchased from the Self Serve Deli cases of a large multi-ethnic market in Los Angeles (Sunland) for 2018 US $4.69 / pound.

Cooking:

  This product is fully cooked and can be cut up and served without further processing - unless it is called for in a recipe. The skin is part of the product as is never removed.

Yield:

  The 1.5 pound half chicken in the photo yielded 1 pound (75%) when de-boned.
