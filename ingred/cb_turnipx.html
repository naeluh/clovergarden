What is a Turnip?


When using cookbooks written in English it can be difficult to know what is meant by "Turnip" because of regional differences in English speaking countries. Asian cookbooks and packaging written in English toss even more confusion into the mix. Illustrated to the left are the terms used in the United States and on the Clovegarden site.
