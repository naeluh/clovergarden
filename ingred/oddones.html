The Odd Ones

This page was for "orphaned" plants that had no relatives of culinary interest. These have all found a place on pages for an appropriate Clade, Division, Order, Family or Genus. The page is still here in case some outside Web sites still have links to it, so links to where its former inhabitants are now can be found easily.   Photo of Ginkgo Leaves in Autumn by Joe Schneid distributed under license Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported.

Arrowhead

- [Kuwai (Japan), Ci gu (China); Sagittaria sagittifolia   |   Duck Potato, Indian Potato, Broadleaf Arrowhead, Wapato: Sagittaria latifolia   |   Sagittaria trifolia]
These have been moved to Alismatales - Water Plantains.

Drumstick Tree

- [Horseradish Tree, Malunggay (Philippines), Sajina (India) Moringa oleifera]
These have been moved to Brassicales.

Ginkgo

- [Ginkgo biloba]
This plant has been moved to Seed Ferns, Ccycads, Ginkgos, etc..

Ginseng

- [Panax quinquefolius (American), Panax ginseng (Korean)]
These have been moved to Parsley & Aralias.

Jujube

- [Red Date, Chinese Date, Annab (Persia (dried)), Taejuja (Korea), Ziziphus zizyphus]
These have been moved to Buckthorns.

Mâche

  -   [Corn Salad, Lamb's Lettuce, Fetticus, Field Salad, Feldsalat, Nut Lettuce, Rapunzel; Valerianella locusta]
This has been moved to Order Dipsacales.

Mangosteen

- [Garcinia mangostana]
This fruit has been moved to Mangosteens.

Mauca

- [Mauka, Chago, Mirabilis expansa]
This plant has been moved to Carnations.

Nettles

- [Stinging Nettles; Urtica dioica]
This plant has been moved to Rosales.

Olluco

- [Papa lisa (South America); Melloco (Ecuador); Olluco (Peru); Chugua (Colombia); Ruba (Venezuela); Ullucus tuberosus]
This plant has been moved to Carnations.

Papaya

- [pawpaw, mamao, lechoza, Carica papaya]
These have been moved to Brassicales.

Poppies

- [Opium Poppy; Papaver somniferum]
These have been moved to Buttercups.

Pineapple

[Ananas comosus]
This plant has been moved to Bromeliads.
