Redbelly Yellowtail Fusilier


[Caesio cuning ]

This Indo-West Pacific fish can grow to nearly 24 inches but is gnerally marketed much smaller. The pictured fish was 12-1/2 inches and weighed 14 ounces. A popular eating fish in the Philippines it can be found in fish markets catering to that community.

More on Fusilier Family.

This fish is quite flat and suitable for broiling and grilling whole or pan dressed.
