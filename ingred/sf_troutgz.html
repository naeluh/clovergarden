Golden Rainbow Trout


[Oncorhynchs mykiss]

This is a color variant of the Rainbow Trout developed by the fish farms and is not to be confused with the real Golden Trout (Oncorhynchus mykiss aguabonita), the California State Fish, which is found in the Kern River drainage region of California.

More on the Trout Family.

This fish is identical to regular rainbow trout in all respects except skin color, so use the cooking information for Rainbow Trout.
