Citrus Juicer

There many kinds and prices for citrus juicers, both hand operated and powered. For normal kitchen work this Oxo unit is my preference. It comes with reamers for Oranges (orange one) and Lemon / Limes (yellow one under orange). I used an Oxo predecessor of this unit for decades, but that design was much harder to clean. Cost was 2022 US $15.99.
