Sieve Set


A set of sieves like this one is very useful where material is pounded in a mortar or on a stone and a specific grain size is desired. After pounding, the material is sieved, and that which doesn't fall through goes back to be pounded more. This set was purchased from an Armenian market in Los Angeles about 40 years ago, and a similar set may be pretty difficult to find in North America today. Many sieve sets are available, but they are "soil sieves", and far too coarse for culinary use.
