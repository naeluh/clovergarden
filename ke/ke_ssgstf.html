Sausage Stuffer

This is a device for real sausage enthusiasts. It is highly recommended over using a meat grinder with the sausage stuffing adapters, as the screw feed of that device is too slow and will heat the meat too much. This model by Desensa can be purchased for about 2022 US $76, and will handle up to 5 pounds of meat. It comes with four stuffing tubes to fit four sizes of casings.
