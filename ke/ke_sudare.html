Sudaré - Sushi Rolling Mat


These mats are used to form sushi rolls. They come in two basic varieties, both shown in the photo. The one fully laid out is made of flat slats, and the folded one on top of it is made of round slats, to provide texture. I'm sure they have other uses, but I don't know what they might be.
