Kitchen Timers

Kitchen timers with alarms can save a lot of "accidents" in a busy kitchen, especially for things in the oven that cannot be seen and are easily forgotten. There are a great many available with a wide range of features, and prices from for $4 to $59.

Kitchen Timer, Counter & Magnetic


Counter-top Timers, often with a back magnet to stick to metal surfaces are available in a very wide range of prices and complexity. The Ozeri unit in the Photo is an example from a bit above the low cost end, about 2020 US $6, and is very easy to use. It can time to just over 1-1/2 hours (99 minutes) and also has a stop watch mode. Similar units that can time more hours cost just a couple dollars more, and 2 timer units can be had for about $12.

At the very high end is the ThermoWorks TimeStack, which has 4 timers that each can time to 99 hours, or act as stop watches. It has a very wide range of advanced features, including recording your own voice alarms so you can hear exactly what has finished cooking without even looking at the timer. Cost is 2020 US $59. Their single timer version also goes to 99 hours and costs 2020 US $29, but doesn't have voice recording.

Kitchen Timer, Pocket


The unit in the photo is a ThermoWorks Time Stick, which has become extremely popular for it's small size (4-1/4 inches long), portability, durability, and ease of use with just one hand. It is much favored for use outdoors, as in competitive cook-offs. By request of their customers they now also make a 3 timer unit, the same shape and just a little bit larger, that is still very easy to use with one hand.

Both versions have very audible alarms, can time to 99 hours, and can also function as stop watches. There are many kitchen timers on the market, but nobody else makes anything like this one. Cost is 2020 US $25, or $35 for the three timer version.
