Korean BBQ Grill Plate

Essential for the famous Korean BBQ, this grill plate is often set on a small butane burner at the table. The pictured unit is 13 inches (33 cm) in overall diameter and sufficient for 2 to 3 people. They're all nonstick these days, but best to get one with a modern three ply PFOA free nonstick.
