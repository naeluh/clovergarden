Roasting Pans


Open V-Rack Roasting Pan

This type of roasting pan is used for regular Wet Cured Hams (City Hams), Chickens, and supermarket Broad Breasted Turkeys. It is not so good for Free Range or Heritage Turkeys, or for Dry Cured Hams (Country Hams). For an example of how this type of roasting pan is used, see our recipe Roasting A Turkey #1.

Covered Roasting Pan

This is the type of roasting pan you want for Heritage and Free Range Turkeys, and for Dry Cured Hams (Country Hams). It can be used also for supermarket Broad Breasted Turkeys and Wet Cured Hams (City Hams), but these days those are usually done in an open V-Rack roasting pan. For an example of how this type of roasting pan is used, see our recipe Roasting A Turkey #2.

Shown here is the classic Granite Ware roasting pan. It is quite superior to shiny stainless steel pans. These come in a variety of sizes. That shown is the 15 inch (about 2022 US $25), which can handle a 7 pound ham, and certainly a large chicken, or a duck, but for a medium turkey, you're going to want the 19 inch model (about 2022 US $40).
