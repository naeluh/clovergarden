Ron Cobb   -   Musica Donavania

It is with great regret that I announce the passing of Ron Cobb from the world of the living in October 2020. Full details are not yet known to me, but he had been in failing health for a few years.


Web Page

Ron Cobb was one of the two true founders of the Chesley Donavan Foundation - from which Musica Donavania is derived. "CD" originally stood for "Cobb Duke Inc.", but was expanded to Chesley Donavan Foundation when others started to join the group.

I met Ron Cobb upon his return from duty in Vietnam, but he never returned to CD meetings. He did attend a couple of Musica Donavania events in October and December of 2000 when he and his wife were visiting from Australia.

I will place a page for Ron Cobb among the "In Memoriam" pages on the Clovegarden.com site, which also hosts the Musica Donavania pages.

More on Ron Cobb can be found on the History of Musica Donavania page.

Contribution of material on the life of Ron Cobb for use on his memoriam page are very welcome. These pages are not obituaries, but celebrations of a person's life, so material submitted can be realistic and not whitewashed.
