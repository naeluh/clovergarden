Make:   -   (30 min)
  1. Rinse CRANBERRIES and pick out any blemished ones.
  2. Grate ORANGE ZEST (orange part only). Squeeze ORANGE JUICE. Slice GINGER very thin, then sliver and chop very fine. Mix all with CINNAMON.
  3. Chop PINEAPPLE very fine.
  4. Bring Orange Juice mix to a slow boil and pour in Cranberries. Simmer the sauce stirring as the berries pop and squish any that fail to pop. Continue to simmer 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in Pineapple and bring back to a simmer. Sweeten to taste with sweetener of choice. Let cool and refrigerate until needed.
NOTES:

  1. Sweetener:

      I've used 5 T (2-1/2 oz) palm sugar, but honey or even plain white sugar can be used depending on your preference. Sweeten to taste but don't over-sweeten.
