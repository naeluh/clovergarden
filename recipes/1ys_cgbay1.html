Yes		 This seasoning is fairly "plug compatible" with Old Bay Seasoning ® popular on the U.S. East Coast for chicken, shrimp and most particularly steamed blue crabs. Ours is a little more aromatic and a little less red than Old Bay.
Note: for seasoning steamed crabs this would be mixed with an equal amount of salt.
