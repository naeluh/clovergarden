This salad is often served with roasted meats. A large Red Onion diced and tumbled in makes an excellent addition.

Make   -   (30 min - 10 min work)
  1. Cut CAULIFLOWER into medium size florets. Place in a steamer basket over boiling water and steam until just tender. Refresh in cold water and drain thoroughly. A salad spinner would work well here.
  2. Mix all Dressing items and tumble with Cauliflower.
  3. Serve cool or at room temperature.
NOTES:

  1. Cauliflower:

      If you wish, you can make this more colorful by mixing some colored cauliflower in with the white.
