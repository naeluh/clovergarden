Make   -   (20 min)
  1. Chop MINT fine. Measure is after chopping, lightly packed.
  2. Chop RED ONION fine.
  3. Whisk together Yogurt, Sour Cream, and Mustard.
  4. Stir in Red Onion, Lemon Juice, and Tabasco.
  5. Season with Salt and Pepper
  6. Fold in Mint.
  7. Packed in a tightly sealed jar and refrigerated, it will last for several days.
NOTES:

  1. Yogurt

      This should be plain whole milk yogurt.
