Make   -   (10 min)

  2. Crush GARLIC and chop very fine.
  3. Mix together all items until evenly distributed. Add another Tablespoon of Water if you want it more liquid.
NOTES:

  1. Merquén

      This is a smoked, moderately hot, chili flake from southern Chile. It includes some salt (merkéen natural) or salt and a small amount of cumin and/or coriander (merkén especial). For details see our Chilis of South America page.
