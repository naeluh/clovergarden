Prep   -   (1 hr)   -  
  1. Chop ONIONS coarse.
  2. Peel CARROTS and dice about 1/4 inch.
  3. Peel POTATOES and cut into about 3/4 inch cubes.
  4. Trim LEEKS to white and light green. Slice about 1/4 inch wide.
  5. Mix all Vegies items,
  6. Scald TOMATOES one minute in boiling water. Quench in cold water and peel. Cut into about 1 inch chunks.
  7. Remove MUSHROOM stems and trim off discolored end. Quarter caps. Mix.
  8. Chop WATERCRESS medium. Weight is as purchased, discard oversized stems.
Run   -   (1 hrs)
  1. Place all Vegies Items in a 3 quart saucepan. Add Water to cover, Butter, Salt, and Pepper. Bring to a boil for 20 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat. Scoop out a little over 1/2 of the Vegetables and purée them in a processor, blender, or mixie. Return purée to the pan.
  3. Stir in Tomatoes and Mushrooms. Bring to a boil, then simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Just Before Serving:   Stir in Cream and Watercress. Do not bring back to a boil. If necessary to reheat this soup, do not bring back to a boil. See also Comments.
NOTES:

  1. Potatoes:

      White Rose or similar work well in recipes of this sort. Avoid Klondike Gold type potatoes - they turn to mush if cooked a little more than the minimum. For details see our Potatoes page.

  2. Comments:

      The pattern recipe was double this size, and just fit an a 5 quart pot - excessive for most of us. "Do not bring to a boil" is standard for soup recipes with cream. I've found that with American heavy whipping cream, if it accidentally boils, the taste is the same, and the appearance is little different.
