Prep   -   (40 min)
  1. Tear ESCAROLE into salad size pieces discarding the thick stems. You should end up with 1-1/4 cup lightly packed.
  2. Discard thick stems and break up WATERCRESS medium. You should have about 2/3 cup lightly packed.
  3. Tear CHICORY leaves into salad size pieces. You should have about 1 cup lightly packed.
  4. Tear ENDIVE leafy parts from stems and into salad size pieces. You should have about 1 cup lightly packed.
  5. Scrape CARROTS, and cut into 2 inch juliennes (I use a julienning vegetable peeler).
  6. Cut SCALLION into 2 inch juliennes.
  7. halve TOMATOES lengthwise and add to the bowl.
  8. Mix All of Above together in a mixing bowl and toss until well distributed.
  9. Hard boil EGG, chill and chop medium for Garnish.
  10. Slice RADISHES very thin for Garnish.
  11. Smash GARLIC and ANCHOVIES to paste in a mortar and whip together All Dressing Items.
  12. At serving time fold in Dressing and sprinkle with Radish Slices and Chopped Egg. For parties see Note-3.
NOTES:

  1. Anchovies:

      Please use Spanish, Italian, or South American anchovies. Moroccan anchovies (95% of canned anchovies) range from poor to disgusting. For details see our Anchovy page.

  2. Sugar:

      This is meant to compensate for all the greens being somewhat bitter, but it is optional depending on your taste.

  3. Buffet Service:

      Make about half again as much dressing as noted. Serve the salad garnished, but with the dressing on the side with a tiny ladle or deep spoon for self service application. Don't put it in something pourable or some bozzo will pour all of it on his one portion.
  4. U.S. measure: t=teaspoon, T=Tablespoon, c=cup, qt=quart, oz=ounce, #=pound, cl=clove in=inch, ar=as required tt=to taste
