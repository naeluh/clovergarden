Prep   -   (25 min)
  1. Tear WATERCRESS into medium size pieces, discarding large stems. Pour into the mixing bowl.
  2. Slice RADISHES very thin and mix with the Watercress.
  3. Pound GARLIC to paste in a mortar. Chop PARSLEY very fine, mix together All Dressing Items.
  4. Fold Dressing into the salad just before serving.
  5. Carefully arrange the salad in individual bowls, seeing that the radishs don't fall to the bottom. Obviously, this salad is for table service, not buffet service.
