There is no standard mix, but what we give here uses the most common ingredients in a fairly common ratio.

Make:   -   (10 min)
  1. Mix all ingredients together. They should be left whole and not ground until time of use.
NOTES:

  1. History:

      Traditionally, cooks in Provence used various herbs in various combinations as they pleased, and used them both dried and fresh. Some mixes, particularly by American companies, now include Lavender, but that wasn't "traditional" even in the 70's formulas.
