Make   -   (2-1/2 hrs - 20 min work)
  1. Score CUCUMBERS (Note-1) lengthwise with the tines of a fork, then slice very thin.
  2. Chop ONION and GINGER very fine and mix with all Dressing items until well blended.
  3. Stir in Cucumber slices until well coated, keep chilled for a couple of hours.
  4. Serve in small bowls with a scattering of snipped chives over.
NOTES:

  1. Cucumbers

      If at all possible, use small diameter unwaxed cucumbers that don't need to be peeled or seeded (Persian, English, Mediterranean, European, Japanese or Kirbys (pickling cukes)).
