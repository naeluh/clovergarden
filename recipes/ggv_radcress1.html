Make:   -   (15 min)
The dressing quantity listed will make a lightly dressed salad, make more if you like a lot of dressing.
  1. Core RADICCHIO and shred medium. Remove any really large stems from WATERCRESS and break up into medium pieces. Mix.
  2. Whip together all Dressing items.
  3. When ready to serve, drizzle dressing over salad and toss lightly.
