A variation, Mustard-Miso Cream Sauce, mixes whipped cream into the mustard sauce, about half and half.

Make:   -   (20 min)
  1. Dry roast SESAME SEEDS until light golden, cool and grind into a paste.For details, see our recipe Toasted Sesame Seeds.
  2. Slowly mix Water into Mustard Powder until the it has the consisntency of regular Dijon mustard.
  3. Mix together ALL Ingredients.
NOTES:

  1. Mustard Powder

      Colman's is fine here.

  2. Shiro Miso

      This is the light colored "white" miso. For details see our Miso Page.
