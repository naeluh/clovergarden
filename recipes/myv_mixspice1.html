Yes		 Many Eastern Mediterranean recipes call for "Mixed Spices". Quite a number of formulas and variations are used, but here are a few very common mixes.

Note:   For any or all of these the whole spices are often dry roasted in an iron pan until aromatic to intensify flavors, as is done in India. Do them each separately because they become aromatic with different time.
