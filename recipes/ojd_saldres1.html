Make:   -   (10 min)
  1. Chop TARRAGON fine. Mix all All items and whip until smooth (I use a mini-prep food processor) and chill well.
  2. Whip again if needed. Made in the mini-prep it will not need to be whipped again, but whipped by hand it might.
NOTES:

  1. Tarragon:

      If you can't get fresh Tarragon, use about three times as much fresh Dill.
