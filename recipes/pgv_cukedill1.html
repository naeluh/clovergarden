Make   -   (1-1/4 hr -12 min work)
  1. Slice CUCUMBERS very thin (less than 1/8 inch thick) on a mandolin or similar device. Put them in a bowl with 1/2 t Salt and tumble so they are evenly coated. Let them sweat for at least 1/2 hour, tumbling a couple of times, then drain thoroughly and squeeze dry. This keeps the salad from getting wattery.
  2. Slice ONION in half lengthwise, then slice crosswise as thin as the cucumbers.
  3. Strip feathery leaves of DILL from the thick stems and chop coarse.
  4. Mix All Items together. Refrigerate for at least an hour.
  5. Serve chilled.
NOTES:

  1. Cucumbers:

      Use narrow unwaxed cucumbers that need not be peeled or seeded (European, Persian, Japanese etc.).

  2. Vinegar:

      Use any kind you like - cider, white wine, even distilled. If you use a more gentle vinegar you can increase to 1/2 cup. I often use a premium quality Philippine cane vinegar (1/2 cup).
