Make   -   (2-1/4 hrs - 10 min work)
  1. Core the CABBAGE and cut lengthwise into narrow slices, then cut the slices to shred about 2 in long. Break up clumps.
  2. Toss Cabbage with Olive Oil to coat, then add Vinegar, Salt and Sugar. Toss thoroughly.
  3. Let sit for 2 hours or more to soften some, tossing occasionally to keep dressing even.
  4. Chill lightly and serve
