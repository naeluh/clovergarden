Make:   -   (15 min)
  1. Grind all Grind items together to a fine powder in your spice grinder (see Note-1).
  2. Mix All items together well.
  3. Well sealed in a Spice Jar away from heat and direct sunlight it will be usable for at least 6 months.
NOTES:

  1. Spice Grinder

      A little whirling blade coffee grinder is excellent for this job - mine is a Krups. Of course you can also do it the traditional way, pounding it in a big stone mortar.
