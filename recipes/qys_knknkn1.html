Make:   -   (15 min)
  1. Grind All Items to a fine powder as needed. Mix All Items and store in a tightly sealed glass jar.

NOTES:

  1. Peanut Flour:

      This is sold in African markets, but you can just grind some Raw Skinned Peanuts fairly fine in a food processor - but watch carefully - If you grind too long it will start to exude oil and become raw Peanut Butter.

  2. Selim Pepper:

      This spice is used mainly in Senegal, but can be found on-line in North America.   Subst:   use 1/2 Cubeb Pepper or Black Pepper, and 1/2 Nutmeg.
