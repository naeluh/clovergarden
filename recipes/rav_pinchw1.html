Yes		 An interesting appetizer with sweetness, tang, and sharpness. Chows (Chow-chows) are usually pickled, but Pineapple is already self pickled.

Make:   -   (20 min + 1 hr marinate)
  1. Peel and Core PINEAPPLE. Cut into about 3/4 inch chunks.
  2. Crush GARLIC and chop very fine
  3. Chop CHILIS fine.
  4. Chop CILANTRO small.
  5. Tumble All Items in a mixing bowl until uniform. Let sit for an hour or so to blend flavors.
  6. Serve chilled.
NOTES:

  1. Chilis, Red:

      Here you have to use your own best judgement as to how many, what size, and how hot. I used two Fresnos with the seed mass removed but veins left in. This provided chili heat that most people are OK with, at least here in Southern California.
