Make:   -   (10 min)
  1. In your Spice Grinder, grind Allspice Berries
  2. Mix All Items well.
  3. Give it a quick whirl in a food processor to make it finer.
  4. In a sealed jar away from heat and sunlight, this mix should be good for at least 6 months.
NOTES:

  1. Chili Powder / Flake:

      This is variable from none (Cuba, Puerto Rico) to plenty (Jamaica, Trinidad & Tobago). Use the amount and hotness you like. This can be Aleppo Extra Hot for mild, ground Japones or Cayenne for medium, and Ground Habanero for very hot.For details, see our Chili Powder & Flake page.

  2. Salt:

      This is highly variable, from none to 1 Tablespoon. Use your own best judgement.
