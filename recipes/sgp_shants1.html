Shan Tofu (1)
-- Garnish
Cilantro Leaves
Toasted Sesame (2)  
-- Dressing
Garlic
Soy Sauce

Shallot Oil (4)

-----------------
Do-Ahead
  1. Make Shan Tofu. Refrigerate 8 hours or overnight - (8 hr - 20 min work)
  2. Toast Toasted Sesame Seeds if you don't have them on hand (10 min).
  3. If you want to use it, make Shallot Oil (25 min to 6 hours- see recipe).
Make:   -   (15 min)
  1. Cut SHAN TOFU as desired and place in individual bowls, or, if serving buffet, in a wide shallow bowl.
  2. Chop CILANTRO small and sprinkle over Salad.
  3. Sprinkle Sesame Seeds over the Salad (don't over-do it).
  4. Crush GARLIC and chop very fine. Mix together all Dressing Items and stir to dissolve Salt.
  5. Drizzle Dressing over Salad and serve cool, but not chilled.
NOTES:

  1. Shan Tofu:

      This "Tofu" is very easy to make - but must be made a day ahead. See our recipe Shan Tofu.

  2. Toasted Sesame Seeds

      This is a pantry staple in Southeast Asia, Korea and Japan. It can be bought in Asian markets, but is better made by our recipe Toasted Sesame Seeds.

  3. Rice Vinegar:

      My favorite by far is Kong Yen Foods Co.'s Aged Gourmet Rice Vinegar from Taiwan.

  4. Shallot Oil:

      This is a Southeast Asian pantry staple. It's easy to make by our recipe Fried Shallots / Shallot Oil recipe. If you don't have it, or don't favor it, use another salad oil, but not Extra Virgin Olive Oil.

  5. Comments:

      The pattern recipe used very finely sliced threads of Kaffir Lime leaves, and suggested Cilantro Leaves as a substitute. Thai Basil leaves would also be very good.
