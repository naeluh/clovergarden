NOTES:

  1. Thai Chili:

      Use your own best judgement here. The amount of Thai Chili can vary from none to 2, though usually there is at least some. For details see our Thai Chilis page.

  2. Fish Sauce:

      This clear liquid is as essential to Southeast Asian cuisine as it was to Imperial Rome. If you are unfamiliar with it, see our Fish Sauce - Introduction page.
