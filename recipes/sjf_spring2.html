Make:   -   (20 min)
  1. Crush GARLIC and chop very fine.
  2. Mix All Ingredients
  3. Let it rest 20 minutes or so before using. Packed in a small tightly covered jar this will keep a month or so in the refrigerator.
NOTES:
  1. Chili Powder:   I use Indian Khandela or Reshempatti which are fairly hot, but in this quantity they gives a chili bite that's fairly mild. Select a powder that gives you the heat you want. For details see our Chili Powders Page.
