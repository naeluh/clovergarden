  2. Mix All Items together and let rest 20 minutes or so, or stir vigorously to get sugar to dissolve. In a small tightly covered jar this will keep a couple of weeks refrigerated.
NOTES:

  1. Vinegar:

      Recipes call for Black Vinegar, White Rice Vinegar, or Red Rice Vinegar. Feel free to choose any of these. I usually use an Aged White Rice Vinegar for dips. For details see our Vinegars & Souring Agents page.

  2. Chilis:

      4 Thai chilis will give this sauce a satisfying bite by Southern California standards. Use your own best judgement here. For details see our Chilis - Thai & Southeast Asia page.
