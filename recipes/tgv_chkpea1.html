Prep   -   (15 min + chill)
  1. If using the egg garnish, hard cook your EGGS, peel, and chill
  2. Drain Chickpeas and rinse.
  3. Chop ONION, RED BELL, GREEN BELL and PARSLEY very small. Mix with Chickpeas.
  4. Whip together all Dressing Items and pour over Salad. Chill well before serving.
  5. Serve garnished with Egg Slices or however you wish.
