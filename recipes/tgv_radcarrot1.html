Make:   -   (45 min - 25 min work)
  1. Peel DAIKON and grate on the course side of your box grater. Peel CARROTS and grate on the fine side. Crush GARLIC and chop very fine. Chop PARSLEY fine.
  2. Mix All Items. Let stand 20 minutes or so turning a couple of times before serving.
  3. Serve chilled or at room temperture.
NOTES:

  1. Daikon Radish:

      Some types of radish used in Anatolia are not much available in North America, but Japanese or Korean Daikon make an acceptable substitute.
