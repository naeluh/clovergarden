Make   -   (30 min)
  1. Peel BEET and dice very fine. Peel ONION and dice very fine. Mix.
  2. Melt Butter in a sauce pan and fry Beet mix stirring until onions are translucent, but no browning (about 5 minutes)
  3. Stir in Vinegar, Salt and Sugar. Reduce over moderately high flame until syrupy.
  4. Cool.
NOTES:

  1. Sugar:

      Scandinavians are way more into sugar than I am, so I have cut the amount in half. Use an amount to your own taste.
